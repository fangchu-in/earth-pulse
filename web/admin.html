Email Sender
Compose and send newsletters and automated alerts to your subscribers. All emails are sent via Resend.com — sign up free (3,000 emails/month free tier).
🤖 Automated email triggers

These emails are sent automatically by a cron job on your Pi or a Cloudflare Worker on a schedule. Requires Resend API key in your .env file.

🐦 New species detected alert
Sent at 4:00 PM daily if a species was detected for the first time this year. Goes to subscribers who opted in for bird alerts.
Enabled — triggers daily at 4 PM if new species detected
🍃 AQI alert
Sent within 1 hour when AQI crosses Good (≤40) or Poor (≥80) threshold. Subscribers choose their threshold in Data Explorer → My Alerts.
Enabled — threshold-based, near real-time
📬 Monthly Earth Pulse report
Sent on the 1st of each month. Top birds detected, AQI trends, monsoon update, hill photo of the month. You compose it below.
Enabled — 1st of each month

To activate these triggers: add RESEND_API_KEY=re_xxxx to your .env file and deploy the included Python trigger script to your Pi's cron.
