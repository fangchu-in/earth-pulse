AI Plant Identification for Baner Hill, Pune

Earth Pulse uses PlantNet (CIRAD · Inria · INRAE · IRD) to identify plants from Baner Hill photos. Conservation profiles are generated by Google Gemini AI (gemini-2.5-flash-lite). Each confirmed identification includes a Gemini-generated description and fun fact stored permanently in the Baner Hill botanical database. GPS-verified identifications within 10km of Baner Hill (18.5526°N, 73.7819°E) are auto-approved. Unverified identifications are queued for admin review before appearing publicly. Baner Hill is located on the Western Ghats foothills, a UNESCO World Heritage biodiversity hotspot adjacent to Pashan Lake, Pune, Maharashtra, India.
