About temperature in Baner, Pune
Data sources: Weather Union ZWL008983 (hyperlocal AWS, Oct 2025+) · Open-Meteo ECMWF (Jan 2022–Sep 2025) · ERA5 reanalysis via Open-Meteo Historical Archive (1980–2021, Copernicus/ECMWF). ERA5 is model-derived — the global standard for climate research, not a direct local sensor measurement. WU observed readings may differ from model data by 2–6°C — this is a real microclimate signal, not an error.
