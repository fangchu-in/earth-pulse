Baner sits at the eastern edge of the Western Ghats, where the Arabian Sea monsoon makes first landfall over the Sahyadri range. The hills act as an orographic rain catcher — most annual rainfall arrives in a concentrated 4-month window.

When the first rain came

The exact day the monsoon first touched Baner — sensor era 2022+
What the data tells us
IMD vs hyperlocal: Baner's ground sensor often detects the first drops 1–2 days ahead of official IMD onset declarations for Pune city. The ERA5 record shows that El Niño years (1987, 1997, 2002, 2015, 2023) consistently have delayed onset — arriving 5–10 days later than average.
